About me
Nice to see you down here!
How did I become a designer? At first I was a banking specialist, then I studied architecture at university. Soon enough I realised there was not much ways to fulfill my ambitions of making the world a better place, until... I have discovered UX design
For the past three years I've been constantly perfecting my skills as an Interface, Product and Lead designer. Nowadays I specialize at complex B2B and B2C systems
I have managed to build two great design teams, not only being a mentor, but a friend for everyone in my crews. In a working process I put human values higher than professional ones because there are much more paths to become effective rather than become empathetic
